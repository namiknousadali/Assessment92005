Climate of New Zealand

General Climate

New Zealand has a largely temperate climate with four distinct seasons {Link: according to Wikipedia https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Climate_of_New_Zealand} [1]. While the far north can experience subtropical weather in summer, and inland alpine areas of the South Island can be very cold in winter, most of the country has mild temperatures, moderate rainfall, and abundant sunshine [1, 3].
